IDCN: Getting internationals into jobs

Helping Internationals in finding a job is an exciting and challenging task. It means integrating versatile talents into a new job market.

In my daily work, I get to know professionals from all kind of backgrounds, spanning from highly specialized Microbiologist over experienced Accountants to polyglot Proofreading specialists.

I joined the International Dual Career Network (IDCN) in December 2017 and have been working with membership data management and strategy development. I am in charge of onboarding new volunteers and initiated a project to review membership data in more effective ways.

Currently, I’m designing a strategic approach for a Mentorship Program where the current volunteers will benefit from the expertise of alumni.
